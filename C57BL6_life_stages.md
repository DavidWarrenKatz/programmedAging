# Life Stages for C57BL/6 Mice

- **Embryogenesis**: Fertilization to E12
- **Fetal Stage**: E12–(21 or 23)
- **Infancy**: Birth to 3 weeks   
- **Childhood**: 3 weeks to 6 weeks 
- **Adolescence**: 6 weeks to 8 weeks 
- **Young Adulthood**: 2 months to 6 months 
- **Adulthood**: 7 months to 14 months
- **Youngest-old**: 15 months to 18 months
- **Old**: 19 months to 24 months
- **Oldest-old**: 25 months to 30 months
- **Centenarians**: 30–36 months
- **Supercentenarians**:  36+ months
